## ðŸŽ¯ Why Range Rings Don't Belong on SD Consoles

### 1. SD vs PPI Display Difference

**PPI (Plan Position Indicator) Radar:**
- Raw radar station displays
- Rotating sweep arm
- Shows raw radar returns (blips)
- Range rings indicate distance from THAT radar
- Used at DEW Line, Pinetree Line, GCI stations

**SD (Situation Display) Console:**
- Computer-processed Direction Center displays
- NO rotating sweep (2.5-second refresh cycle)
- Shows correlated tracks from MULTIPLE radars
- Distance is ambiguous (tracks from 28 different radar stations)
- Range rings would be meaningless (range from WHERE?)
